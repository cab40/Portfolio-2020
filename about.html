Hi there! I'm Catherine

I am a Systems Design Engineering Student at the University of Waterloo (graduating class of 2025). Now you may be thinking “what exactly is Systems Design Engineering?”. Well, we employ a systems approach to design. Looking at a given system, an interacting combination of software, people, machines, and tools, as a whole we create problem-based solutions using principles of engineering, UI/UX design, and creativity.

I often find people frustrated by technologies they encounter in life simply because they are too difficult to use. However, there are exceptions where the from and function of the technology works beautifully together making it simple, intuitive, and a pleasure to use. I want to apply my creative skills to the development of software and systems in a way that is easier, more accessible, and fun for individuals; effectively becoming a leader in user-centered innovation.
